Screen-Based Media Design

Equip yourself with the ability to identify screen-based media works and experiment with the creative potential of moving image and interactive technologies.

Course Objectives

This subject aims to equip students with the ability to identify a wide range of screen-based media works and experiment with the creative potential of moving image. From early cinema to multi-media arts, students will acquire conceptual knowledge for evaluating and analysing aesthetic achievement and theory of individual works of different styles, genres, and narrative structures. With the purpose of enhancing students’ skills and artistic sensibility for producing high quality screen-based works, practical workshops on time-based audio-visual creation and contemporary media art practices are provided.

Subject Intended Learning Outcomes:

  • Interpret the influence of technology on the evolution of screen-based media.
  • Discuss the relationship between various screen-based practices and how visual perception is formed.
  • Evaluate the social, cultural, political and psychological influence on the development of screen-based media.
  • Design creative works by utilising narrative techniques in considering the engagement of spectators.
